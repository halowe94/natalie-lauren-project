Spending Time with NL

Spending time with Natalie's art took me on a journey of reflection, meditation, and unfiltered existence. She navigates joy and sorrow as well as the complexity between them. In her work, I find that I seen, my reality is captured, and I desire to live into the fullness of what it means to be human.

How to Engage

I invite you to engage where my exploration has taken me. In this website you will find: the theological themes in her work, several quotes form Natalie Lauren, and a poem I have created to process my expreience. There is also a page where you can connect with more of her work.
